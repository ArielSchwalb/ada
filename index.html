Ada Lovelace

The First Ever Computer Programmer

Here's a time line of Ada Lovelace's life:

  • 1815 - Born in London, England
  • 1835 - Married William, 8th Baron King, and became Lady King.
  • 1840s - Studied advanced calculus. She was encouraged to learn mathematics by her mother, who wanted to ensure that she grew up to be more logical than her father.
  • 1842 - Translated, edited, and contributed to a published paper which ended up in Scientific Memoirs. The first computer algorithm ever written, which was never tested, was one of her notes in the paper.
  • 1851 - Died from cancer. Her husband left her on her death bed.
